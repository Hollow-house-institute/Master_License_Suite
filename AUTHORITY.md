# AUTHORITY

## Human Authority Declaration

The Master License Suite is governed under explicit human authority.

All licensing, governance, enforcement, and interpretive decisions are
ultimately accountable to a designated Human Authority acting on behalf of
Hollow House Institute (HHI).

Automation, tooling, and documentation do not possess autonomous authority.

---

## Authority Order

Authority within this repository follows the order below and may not be
reversed or bypassed:

1. **Human Authority**
2. Governance Standards
3. Licensing Instruments
4. Automation and Tooling

Human Authority retains final decision rights over all lower layers.

---

## Human Authority Role

The Human Authority is responsible for:

- Approving governance changes
- Approving enforcement bindings
- Interpreting license intent
- Authorizing exceptions or remediation
- Initiating or halting enforcement actions

Human Authority decisions are binding even when automation or documentation
suggests alternative outcomes.

---

## Non-Delegation Clause

Authority may not be fully delegated to:

- Automated systems
- Continuous integration pipelines
- AI agents
- External contributors
- Majority consensus mechanisms

Human Authority review is required for all governance-relevant changes.

---

## Repository Controls

This repository enforces human authority through:

- Protected branches
- Required approving reviews
- CODEOWNERS enforcement
- Signed commits
- Pull-request-only changes

Direct pushes to protected branches are intentionally disallowed.

---

## Audit and Traceability

All authority decisions must be traceable through:

- Pull request history
- Review records
- Signed commits
- Explicit documentation

Silence, automation, or historical precedent do not constitute approval.

---

## Scope

This authority declaration applies specifically to:

- The Master License Suite
- All licenses, bindings, and enforcement logic contained herein
- Downstream repositories that explicitly reference this suite

---

## Statement of Intent

This repository is designed to demonstrate governance that is:

- Human-centered
- Enforceable
- Auditable
- Resistant to silent drift

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.
Behavior is the most honest data there is.
