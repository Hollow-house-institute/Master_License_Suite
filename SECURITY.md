# Security & Responsible Use Policy

## Scope

This policy governs security, misuse, and vulnerability reporting related to all materials licensed under the **Hollow House Institute Master License Suite (HHI-MLS)**.

This includes datasets, schemas, documentation, governance frameworks, and derivative artifacts.

---

## Prohibited Activities

The following are considered **security violations and license breaches**:

- Unauthorized commercial use
- AI model training, fine-tuning, embedding, or RAG use
- Dataset aggregation or redistribution
- Derivative system construction
- Identity, behavioral, or psychological exploitation
- Attempts to bypass access controls or license terms

---

## Vulnerability & Misuse Reporting

If you identify:
- License circumvention
- Unauthorized usage
- Data misuse
- Security vulnerabilities
- Ethical or governance risks

Report immediately to:  
**data@hollowhouse.org**

Include:
- Description of the issue
- Affected materials
- Evidence (links, hashes, screenshots if applicable)

---

## Disclosure Policy

- Hollow House Institute will acknowledge reports within **72 hours**
- Coordinated disclosure timelines are determined case-by-case
- Public disclosure without authorization may constitute a license violation

---

## Reservation of Rights

Hollow House Institute reserves the right to:
- Revoke access
- Terminate licenses
- Pursue remedies under applicable law
- Enforce governance restrictions
