# Counsel Brief — Hollow House Institute Master License Suite

## Purpose

This repository establishes a private licensing and governance authority for sensitive behavioral, relational, and longitudinal AI materials.

The objective is to:
- Prevent unauthorized AI training and derivative exploitation
- Maintain longitudinal behavioral integrity
- Enforce ethical and identity-protective constraints
- Provide regulator- and enterprise-readable governance artifacts

---

## Structure

The system consists of:

1. **HHI-LIC-01** — Master Licensing & Governance Standard  
2. **Master License Suite Repository** — Canonical authority  
3. **Individual License Instruments** — Material-specific constraints  
4. **Security & Enforcement Policy** — Misuse and breach handling  

All materials are **restricted-access by default**.  
No implied rights are granted.

---

## Key Legal Posture

- Non-commercial by default (broadly defined)
- Explicit prohibition on:
  - AI training
  - Fine-tuning
  - Embedding
  - RAG
  - Derivative exploitation
- Attribution required
- Rights reserved unless explicitly granted in writing

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- License termination
- Access revocation
- Legal remedies
- Cross-jurisdictional enforcement

## Intended Review Outcome

This suite is designed to:
- Withstand enterprise legal review
- Be citeable in regulatory or audit contexts
- Function as a private governance standard
